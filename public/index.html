  1. Start: Each player gets 4 face-down cards. Peek at your bottom 2 cards for 5 seconds.
  2. Your Turn: Draw from deck or discard pile, then either:
    • Replace a card in your hand
    • Discard a power card to use its ability
  3. Match: Tap any card that matches the discard pile to eliminate it! Match opponent's cards to give them a penalty.
  4. KYRO: Call KYRO to end the round when you think you have the lowest score. ‚ö†Ô∏è If you're wrong, your points DOUBLE!
