HOW TO PLAY

  • GOAL: Lowest score wins. First to 50 loses.
  • START: Peek at your bottom 2 cards (5 seconds).
  • DRAW: Take from deck or discard pile.
  • MATCH: Tap ANY card matching discard to eliminate it. Match opponent's cards to give them a penalty!
  • POWERS: 11=Peek your card, 12=Spy opponent's card, 13=Swap any 2 cards, 0=Swap any 2 cards.
  • SCORING: Lower is better! -1 (best), 1-3 (good), 4-8 (ok), 9-13 (bad).
  • KYRO: End round if you think you're winning. If you DON'T have the lowest score, your points DOUBLE!
